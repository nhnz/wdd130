Purpose

We provide chess lovers around the world with the upcoming chess events schedule. We take you through tournament preparations to the big day of the tournament.

Content

Home

The website will be filled with a lot of exciting information for chess lovers, mainly information about big upcoming events like world championships and many more. We will offer dates, times, and venues for each event. You will know who will be participating and what criteria are used to qualify each participant. We will share the prizes to be won will be shared and the rules of each event. The rules of each event differ every time. Some events have no time constraints, and some events do. Knowing this beforehand helps the attendees anticipate what kind of strategies the players may use in the tournaments. . We offer information from juniors to grandmaster competitions both male and female competitions. All ages are catered for. Kids witness their favorite player rise from juniors to the professional league. We know that chess tournaments happen almost every day so we will create a platform for our website users to inform us of the tournaments that we might have missed or tournaments that they have organized that they want to invite people to.

Learn

We will let you know of each player’s favorite opening moves, and you predict what opening you think your favorite player might use. A brief history of each participant will be offered, and their position on the world ranking shared. Not only does the site offer you information about future events but you will also learn about the past great moments of chess, some of the great world champions’ life history, world championship matches, and chess equipment history. Enjoy the journey as we put the pieces of the past together, the pieces that got us to modern chess. With us, you will not only know what events are coming but you will also sharpen your chess skills and knowledge. Our site does not offer an online gaming platform but offers information about the ins and outs of the chess world. Our site is more focused on you as a chess fan, updating you with relevant new information about what is happening in the chess world right now. Take it as a chess newsroom version where current chess affairs are presented to you. We are planning to make a website with long boring textual information, we want our site to be interactive.

Fun and Games

Not all the events allow spectators in the event venue but with us, you need not worry we will provide a link you can use to watch online. In the events to which spectators are invited, we will have challenges and the winner will win a ticket to go watch the event and meet his or her favorite player. Predict the tournament winner and win cool chess prizes. Learning chess could be fun we plan to create a children’s page to help them learn chess, know how each piece moves, and have quizzes they can play to help them learn chess. Not only this page could be for kids, but it could prove very useful for those who wish to learn chess even in their adult state. Any chess lover can enter and view our site and test his or her knowledge, but only registered members can win the prizes. Registration is free. Only the full name of the person and email address are needed to register. We put the fun in learning for both children and adults. The plan is to make the site for families and individuals, have fun times with your kids learning chess facts and answering quizzes, and win prizes for your kids or even yourself. Put yourself in a chance to of winning tickets to go watch the matches live in the event.
